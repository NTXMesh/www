Devices for the Mesh

NTX mesh uses two technologies called Meshtastic and Meshcore to power the mesh. In order to connect to this network, a relatively low cost device is required. You'll often hear these devices referred to as nodes.

  • Device Options
    • Pocket/Portable nodes
    • Stationary/Solar nodes
