    DFW / North Texas Mesh (NTX Mesh)

    NTX Mesh is a community group working to build a mesh network of solar-powered Meshtastic radios in DFW and the greater North Texas area. This network acts like a city-wide text messaging system, allowing people to communicate publicly or privately with anyone on the network. All of this happens without any external infrastructure - no power, no cell phone towers, no internet.
